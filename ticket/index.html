Setup-Ticket System

How to CREATE the Ticket-System❓

Use /giveaway-create; - [channel] - [category] - [title] - [description] - [ticket-title] - [ticket-description] - [handlers] - (button-name) - (button-emoji) - (transcripts) ❗

TIP: And in '[ticket-title]' you can enter '{name}' for the name of who created the ticket, and also in '[ticket-description]' you can enter {space} for an 'ENTER-SPACE' use ❗

What is the Ticket-Channel name 📝

The Ticket-Channel is ╰🎫｜ticket-{id} and the id is taken from the Discord user-id of the person who started the ticket, and the first '4' numbers are taken from this.

(e.g; 12345678 is the ticket name '╰🎫｜ticket-1234')
